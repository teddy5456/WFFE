Product Story

Our Premium Blue Velvet Sofa represents the pinnacle of Kenyan craftsmanship, blending traditional techniques with contemporary design. Each piece is hand-upholstered by our team of expert artisans in Nairobi, using only the finest materials sourced from ethical suppliers across East Africa.

Key Features

  • Frame: Kiln-dried hardwood from sustainable forests in the Aberdare Mountains
  • Upholstery: Luxurious 100% polyester velvet with stain-resistant Nano-Tex treatment
  • Cushioning: High-resiliency foam core wrapped in feather down for perfect comfort
  • Dimensions: 72"W x 36"D x 32"H (3-seater version)
  • Weight Capacity: 750 lbs distributed weight
  • Legs: Solid acacia wood with optional brass or ebony finishes

Care Instructions

For daily maintenance, gently vacuum with a brush attachment. Spot clean velvet with a mild detergent and cold water using a soft cloth, always brushing in the direction of the pile. Professional cleaning recommended annually to maintain fabric's luxurious feel. Rotate cushions monthly for even wear.

Do

  • Blot spills immediately with clean cloth
  • Use a velvet brush to restore pile
  • Keep out of direct sunlight

Don't

  • Use harsh chemicals or steam cleaners
  • Rub stains vigorously
  • Place near heat sources

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible manufacturing:

  • FSC-certified hardwood frames
  • OEKO-TEX certified fabrics free from harmful chemicals
  • Natural latex cushions instead of synthetic foams
  • Biodegradable packaging materials
  • Carbon-neutral shipping within Kenya

How does the velvet hold up with pets?

Asked by James M. on June 15, 2023 2 answers
WFFE Customer Care Official Response

Our performance velvet is specifically treated to resist pet hair and minor scratches. The tight weave makes it difficult for claws to catch, and any marks can usually be brushed out with the nap. We recommend keeping your pet's nails trimmed and providing alternative scratching surfaces.
